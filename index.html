Whiteboard Animation

Draw something on the whiteboard below, then click "Animate" to see how the computer reconstructs your drawing stroke by stroke.

How it works (The Logic)

1. Recording: As you draw, we capture the X and Y coordinates of your mouse movement into an array of "paths".
2. Replay: When you click animate, we clear the canvas.
3. Animation: We loop through those saved coordinates and redraw the lines frame-by-frame, moving the hand image to the current coordinate.
